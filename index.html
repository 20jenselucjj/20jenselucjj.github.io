Project THOR

Computer Swap

THOR is a comprehensive initiative to replace and recycle the old systems in the city.

• To ensure the security and confidentiality of our data by destroying the hard drives of the old systems using approved methods. • To enhance the performance and efficiency of our operations by upgrading each department with new system computers that meet their specific software requirements. • To standardize and streamline our IT infrastructure by reimaging all the new system computers with Windows 10. • The project is executed in phases, starting with an inventory and assessment of the current systems, followed by a procurement and installation of the new systems, and finally a disposal and recycling of the old systems. The project will involve close collaboration between the IT department and each business unit to ensure a smooth transition and minimal disruption.
