Project THOR

Computer Swap

THOR is a comprehensive initiative to replace and recycle the old systems in the city.

  • To ensure the security and confidentiality of our data by destroying the hard drives of the old systems using approved methods.
  • To enhance the performance and efficiency of our operations by upgrading each department with new system computers that meet their specific software requirements.
  • To standardize and streamline our IT infrastructure by reimaging all the new system computers with Windows 10.

The project is executed in phases, starting with an inventory and assessment of the current systems, followed by a procurement and installation of the new systems, and finally a disposal and recycling of the old systems. The project will involve close collaboration between the IT department and each business unit to ensure a smooth transition and minimal disruption.

Time Period: November 2022 - Present

Device Renaming Project

The Device Renaming Project was an independent project given to me that aimed at changing the device names of all laptops, computers, and tablets in your organization. The project was focused on ensuring that each device had a specific department and asset code as part of the name, making it easier to track and manage the devices.

  • The benefits of the project were significant. The new naming convention made it easier to track and manage devices, reducing the risk of loss or theft.
  • It also improved the efficiency of device management, making it easier to assign devices to new staff members or departments as needed.
  • Overall, the Device Renaming Project was a critical initiative that improved the overall efficiency and security of your organization. By implementing a standardized naming convention for all devices, your organization was better positioned to manage your technological infrastructure and meet the evolving needs of your business.

Time Period: May 2022 - August 2022
